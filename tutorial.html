Empire of Forth v.1.0

If you want to practise your fighting with the experience you gathered out in the wild, you can teleport here at any moment while you aren't under attack. You also got yourself a second teleport-crystal to teleport to the smaller village that is living near camp, but the crystal isn't connected yet.

Item Full name Description
ATK Attack This describes your physical force. It determines your damage, but also how much you get affected by healing potions.
DEF Defence This determines your ability to block attacks. After blocking an opponents attack your next attack will be a little stronger.
HP Hitpoints This is your physical ability to stay alive. Don't let it reach zero, or else you won't be no more.
XP Experience points As the name suggests, this is the experience you gained on your adventure. It determines when you can increase one of your stats.
AP Ability points Sounds similiar to XP, doesn't it? This number determines how much you can increase one of your other stats. You gain one after each level up
GP Gold pieces Rather simple, innit? These are your coins you have in your purse. I heard you can get rid of some gold in a village nearby, if you want to.

Now go. You got an empire to save, don't you?
